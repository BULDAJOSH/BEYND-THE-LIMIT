About Beyond The Limit

Beyond The Limit is a streetwear brand built on motivation, ambition, and self-belief. It represents people who push beyond limits and continue chasing their dreams despite challenges.

Our Story

This brand started as a small idea from a first-year college student in Imus, Cavite. It grew from passion for fashion and the desire to inspire others through clothing that represents strength and determination.

Mission

To inspire individuals to go beyond their limits through fashion and self-expression.

Vision

To become a globally recognized streetwear brand representing confidence, faith, and perseverance.
